🎣 Reel Adventure A BRAINY FISHING GAME

How to play & what's in the sea

Move your mouse or finger to aim where the line should land (watch the target ring), then click or tap to cast. The hook sails through the air, splashes down, and sinks — touch a fish to hook it. Press and hold to pause the lure at any depth (great for waiting out a fish); let go to reel it back up — no need to hit the bottom. It catches on the way down AND on the way back up! In math modes you solve a quick puzzle to land it; in Just Fishing you simply reel it in. Harder, deeper, faster catches score more.

Hook a big fighter — a 🦈 shark, tuna, 🐊 alligator, or storm-borne 🐉 sea serpent — and you'll tap REEL to wrestle it aboard. Yank too hard, though, and a monster can flip your boat — and that's game over! The ⏸️ Pause button freezes everything — and lets you end the round whenever you like.

🌊 Protected species: 🐬 Dolphins, 🐳 Orcas, 🐋 Blue Whales and 🐢 Sea Turtles are protected — just like in the real ocean. Hook one and you choose: release it (no penalty — it leaps away with a happy splash! 🥰) or keep it, which costs you points. Always let them go!

⭐ XP Stars upgrade your rod! Fish them up — and ♻️ haul up trash (🥾 🥫) for double XP — to earn sturdier rods (🎋 → 🎣 → 🛠️ → 🔱) that reel faster and resist capsizing, so you can land 🐉 sea serpents. Your rod resets each game, so power up fast!

🐟 Minnow +5🐠 Clownfish +10🦀 Crab +10 🦞 Lobster +15🐡 Pufferfish +20🐙 Octopus +25 🦑 Squid +30🐟 Tuna +35🦈 Shark +40 🐊 Alligator +48🌟 Golden +50💎 Treasure bonus! ⭐ XP Star — upgrade your rod!

Watch out: 🪼 Jellyfish sting (−5s). 🥾 boots & 🥫 cans cost a few points — but cleaning them up earns ♻️ double XP for your rod, so trash is a tradeoff!

⛈️ Storms roll in now and then — a rainstorm (rain & lightning), a wind storm (currents that drag your lure sideways), or, rarely, a roaring hurricane with a swirling whirlpool. They bring fiercer, faster creatures (even a 🐉 sea serpent and a ⚡ electric eel) and a pitching boat that's far easier to capsize — but every catch is worth double! Watch the sky: lightning can even strike your boat and knock your rod overboard. Storms blow over in about 20 seconds.
